Travel To Hue City

Introduction

Hue, located in Thua Thien-Hue province in central Vietnam, is an ancient imperial city known for its rich cultural heritage, featuring the UNESCO-recognized Imperial Citadel, royal tombs, pagodas, and its role as the former capital of the Nguyen Dynasty.
